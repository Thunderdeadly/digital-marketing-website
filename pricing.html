FAQ

Questions, answered

Is there a long-term contract?

No. Every plan is month-to-month. We earn your business each month with results, not with a contract that traps you.

What's included in the free audit?

A senior strategist reviews your website, ads, funnels and competitors, then sends back a teardown of what's working, what's leaking money, and the three fastest wins. It's yours to keep whether or not you work with us.

Does the price include ad spend?

No — the monthly fee covers our strategy and management. Your ad budget is paid directly to the platforms (Google, Meta, etc.) and stays in your own accounts.

How soon will I see results?

Paid channels can move within weeks; SEO and content compound over months. Your strategy call sets realistic timelines and targets up front — no vague promises.

Can I switch plans later?

Anytime. Most clients start with one or two channels and scale up as the numbers prove out. Upgrades and downgrades take effect the next billing cycle.

Who actually does the work?

Senior specialists on our team — not junior staff or offshore contractors handed a checklist. You'll know exactly who's on your account.
